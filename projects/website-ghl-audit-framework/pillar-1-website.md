# Website Audit Methodology

## Per-page review rubric

| Dimension | What to check |
|-----------|---------------|
| Lead capture | Forms, CTAs, chat widgets — do they exist and work? |
| Funnel clarity | Can a visitor understand the offer in 5 seconds? |
| Mobile optimization | Is the experience degraded on phone? |
| Page speed | Load time, Core Web Vitals |
| SEO fundamentals | Title tags, meta, headers, schema |
| Trust signals | Testimonials, certifications, case studies |
| Content accuracy | Office hours, contact info, service descriptions |
| Compliance language | Required disclaimers, privacy policy, terms |

## Scoring

Each finding ranked by revenue impact:

| Score | Revenue Impact | Effort |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| 🔴 Critical | Direct revenue loss >20% | Days |
| 🟡 High | 10-20% revenue impact | 1-2 weeks |
| 🟢 Medium | Efficiency gain | 2-4 weeks |
| ⚪ Low | Nice-to-have, cosmetic | 4+ weeks |

## Top lead-leakage points (typical)

1. No chat widget or bot on high-intent pages
2. Forms that don't trigger CRM actions
3. Missing follow-up after form submission
4. Booking requires 3+ touchpoints
5. Office hours mismatch with customer behavior
6. No mobile-friendly call-to-action
7. Slow page load on key landing pages
8. No social proof near conversion points
9. FAQ has answers that should be automated
10. Compliance disclaimers missing from lead-gen pages
