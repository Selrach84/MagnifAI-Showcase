      Destinations

      Browsers can't push RTMP, so streaming runs server-side via Jitsi Jibri (JaaS or self-host). Single = Jitsi streams to one platform. Relay = Jitsi streams to your relay which fans out to all platforms — use the generated ffmpeg/nginx config on your relay box, or a service like Restream/Cloudflare. Start/stop the stream from the in-call 📡 button. Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn/X give a per-broadcast Server URL + key — paste them.
