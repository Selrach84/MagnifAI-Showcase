# Roofing Company Automation OS

> A complete technical foundation for a storm-restoration roofing company — CRM, AI claims triage, automated lead gen, phone system, and a scalable service package for other contractors.

**Stack:** GoHighLevel · AI Estimator · AI Phone System · Google Ads · Competitive Intelligence

## The problem

A well-established roofing company ($35K avg job, insurance-based storm restoration) had zero technical infrastructure. No marketing CRM, no lead gen system, no automated follow-ups, no competitive intelligence. Previous agencies failed to deliver. Every claim was reviewed manually (30-40 min each). Referral partners were managed via ad-hoc calls and texts.

## The solution

A 12-week phased build transforming the company into a tech-enabled operation:

### Phase 1a: Foundation (Weeks 1-3)

| Priority | Component | Details |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| 🥇 | GoHighLevel CRM | Lead capture forms, pipeline stages, automated follow-up sequences, SMS/email nurture |
| 🥇 | Website | Modern, mobile-first, optimized for storm-damage leads |
| 🥇 | Google Ads | Targeted campaigns geotargeted to storm zones |

**Result:** Leads visible within 2-3 weeks.

### Phase 1b: AI Automation (Weeks 4-6)

| Priority | Component | Details |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| 🥇 | AI Claims Triage | Auto-review insurance claims in 5 min vs 30-40 min, prioritize qualified leads |
| 🥇 | Lead Qualification Bot | Website + SMS bot that captures, qualifies, and books inspections automatically |
| 🥈 | Automated Follow-Ups | Post-estimate, post-job, referral-request sequences on autopilot |

**Result:** Claims processing 3x faster.

### Phase 1c: Intelligence & Scale (Weeks 7-9)

| Priority | Component | Details |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| 🥇 | Competitive Intelligence | Monitor competitor social media for market signals, project wins, hiring |
| 🥇 | Dashboard | Real-time view: leads, claims pipeline, ad ROI, competitor moves |
| 🥈 | Referral Partner Network | Automated system for insurance agents — drip campaigns, referral tracking |

**Result:** Full command center — run the business from a phone.

### Phase 1d: Polish & Showcase (Weeks 10-12)

| Priority | Component | Details |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| 🥇 | Case Study Package | Before/after metrics: lead volume, response time, claims speed, ad ROI |
| 🥇 | Demo System | Living demo showing how the system runs |
| 🥈 | Service Menu & Pricing | Defined packages with results expectations |

**Result:** A showcase-ready system to sell to other contractors.

## Key design decisions

- **Insider-led sales**: The client's industry credibility (15 years as top adjuster) is the sales moat — the tech enables what they already know
- **Proof-of-concept first**: Build the complete system on the client's own company, then use it as a showcase to sell to peers
- **Turnkey requirement**: Client is non-technical — everything must be dashboard-driven, zero configuration
- **Existing IP reuse**: AI claims triage, competitive intel "spy," and GHL bots were pre-built — directly applicable
- **Single system wedge**: Not a la carte services — one complete turnkey operation is the product

## What this demonstrates

- Full GHL ecosystem implementation (CRM, bots, pipelines, sequences)
- AI-powered operational efficiency (5-min claims triage vs 30-min manual)
- Phased delivery methodology with milestones and measurable outcomes
- Sell-what-you-build strategy — the system is both the deliverable and the sales tool
- Capacity to package and productize automation for an entire industry vertical
